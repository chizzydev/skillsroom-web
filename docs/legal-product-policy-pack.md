# Skill Rooms Legal + Product Policy Pack

Updated: June 2, 2026

## Purpose

This pack defines the public-facing policy baseline that Skill Rooms should present before launch. It is written as product policy for closed beta and launch preparation, not as final legal advice.

## Public Policy Surfaces

- `/policies`
- `/terms`
- `/rules`
- `/prizes`
- `/refunds`
- `/disputes`
- `/conduct`
- `/eligibility`
- `/privacy`
- `/compliance`
- `/trust`
- `/support`

## Core Policy Decisions

- Skill Rooms is positioned as a skill-based competitive gaming platform, not a chance-based betting or casino product.
- Public and prize-bearing participation should be treated as `18+` until a separate reviewed youth framework exists.
- Reserved and queued money language is allowed only when backed by real ledger states.
- Completed payout claims should remain hidden until provider-approved reconciliation exists.
- Terms, refund, dispute, privacy, and compliance language should receive formal legal review before public launch.

## UX Expectations

- Policy pages must be public and readable without login.
- The policy hub should act as the main discovery page for legal/product policy.
- Footer navigation should expose the policy hub and key policy routes.
- Copy should stay calm, operational, and free from betting/casino tone.

## Follow-up Before Public Launch

- Formal legal review for Nigerian launch.
- Payment-provider review of the competition and settlement model.
- Final support contact and escalation process.
- Country expansion review before enabling new markets.
